Privacy Policy

This extension, AIUB CAPTCHA Solver, does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

All operations are performed locally in the user's browser and apply only to the AIUB university portal at https://portal.aiub.edu.

The extension does not use any tracking, analytics, or third-party scripts. It respects user privacy and data security at all times.
